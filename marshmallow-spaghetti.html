Marshmallow Spaghetti

Marshmallow Spaghetti is a whimsical and unexpected dish that combines traditional spaghetti with an array of playful toppings. The spaghetti is coated in a pinkish sauce made from mini marshmallows and spaghetti sauce, then drizzled with chocolate syrup. It's topped with grated cheddar cheese, colorful gummy bears, sliced dill pickles, and garnished with crushed potato chips. This vibrant and fun recipe is sure to delight and confuse your taste buds in the most delightful way!

Prep Time
10 minutes
Cook Time
30 minutes
Servings
4

Ingredients
  • 1 pound of spaghetti noodles
  • 3 cups of mini marshmallows (the more colorful, the better)
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce (preferably the one with the meatballs already in it)
  • 2 cups of chocolate syrup
  • 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese
  • 4 dill pickles, sliced
  • 1 bag of gummy bears
  • 1 teaspoon of hot sauce
  • 2 cups of orange juice
  • A handful of potato chips (for garnish)

Instructions
  1. Boil the Spaghetti: Start by bringing a large pot of water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt, a splash of orange juice for that zesty twist, and the spaghetti noodles. Cook until the noodles are al dente.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: While the noodles are cooking, pour the jar of spaghetti sauce into a separate pot. Add the mini marshmallows and stir over medium heat until the marshmallows start to melt and blend with the sauce, creating a lovely pinkish hue. Stir in the hot sauce for an unexpected kick.
  3. Combine and Cook: Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it and return it to the pot. Pour the marshmallow-spaghetti sauce over the noodles and mix well. Add the chocolate syrup and continue to stir until everything is evenly coated. Trust me, the chocolate syrup is the secret ingredient that turns this dish from "meh" to "whoa!"
  4. Add the Toppings: Now it's time for the toppings! Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese over the spaghetti. Add the sliced dill pickles and gummy bears. The gummy bears add a chewy texture that really surprises the palate. If youâ€™re feeling adventurous, crush a handful of potato chips and sprinkle them on top for that extra crunch.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the Marshmallow Spaghetti on a large plate. Make sure to garnish with a few more potato chips and maybe a drizzle of chocolate syrup for presentation.
  6. Pro Tip: For an extra special touch, pair this dish with a glass of chilled orange juice. The citrus notes will cut through the richness of the sauce, balancing out the flavors and leaving your taste buds both confused and delighted.

Notes

  • For an even more adventurous dish, try adding different types of gummy candies.
  • This recipe is a great conversation starter and perfect for themed parties or quirky dinner nights.
  • Adjust the amount of hot sauce based on your spice tolerance.
